PocketChampion Open Beta Announcement

March 15, 2026

Dear Trainers:

We are excited to announce that PocketChampion will officially launch its open beta on March 20, 2026! After a long period of development and testing, we are finally ready to present this game to everyone.

Open Beta Content

  • 1-9 Generations 1000+ Pokemon collection, including all starters and legendary Pokemon
  • Terastalization + Mega Evolution coexistence, new battle experience
  • 3D HD/pixelated free switching, battle perspective free adjustment
  • Two-player battles, multiplayer Terastal battles, new battle gameplay
  • Rich game story and mission system
  • Pokemon training and evolution system

Open Beta Benefits

To celebrate the open beta, we have prepared generous benefits:

  • All open beta players will receive a newbie gift package: 1000 Diamonds, 10 Advanced Poke Balls, 50 Experience Candies
  • Log in for 7 consecutive days to get a rare Pokemon
  • Complete newbie tasks to get a Mega Stone
  • Participate in open beta activities to get limited skins and titles

Download Methods

Players can download the game through the following methods:

  • Official website: https://game.6266game.cn/website/zdbjl/?w7cdSjjj
  • Search for "PocketChampion" in major app stores

We look forward to adventuring with you in the world of PocketChampion, collecting Pokemon, challenging opponents, and becoming the strongest trainer!

PocketChampion Development Team

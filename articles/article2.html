Starter Pokemon Selection Guide

March 10, 2026

In PocketChampion, players can choose one starter Pokemon from generations 1-9. Different Pokemon have different attributes and skills. Choosing the right starter can make your adventure smoother. This guide introduces the characteristics and pros/cons of each generation's starters to help you make the best choice.

Generation 1 Starters

Charmander (Fire Type)

Charmander is a Fire-type Pokemon that evolves into Charizard. Its advantage lies in high attack power and speed, suitable for players who prefer fast attacks. Charizard can Mega Evolve into Charizard X (Fire+Dragon) or Charizard Y (Fire+Flying), further enhancing its abilities.

Squirtle (Water Type)

Squirtle is a Water-type Pokemon that evolves into Blastoise. Its advantage lies in high defense and endurance, suitable for players who prefer defensive strategies. Blastoise can Mega Evolve to further enhance its defensive capabilities.

Bulbasaur (Grass Type)

Bulbasaur is a Grass-type Pokemon that evolves into Venusaur. Its advantage lies in balance and versatility, suitable for players who prefer balanced strategies. Venusaur can Mega Evolve to further enhance its abilities.

Selection Tips

When choosing your starter, consider the following factors:

  • Your preferred playstyle (offensive, defensive, or balanced)
  • The Pokemon you plan to collect later
  • The advantages and disadvantages of each type
  • Mega Evolution and Terastalization abilities

Remember, there is no "right" or "wrong" choice. The most important thing is to choose the Pokemon that best suits your playstyle and makes you enjoy the adventure.

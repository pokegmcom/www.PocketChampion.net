Terastalization System Guide

March 5, 2026

Terastalization is an important battle mechanic in PocketChampion that can change a Pokemon's type and enhance specific moves. This guide provides a detailed introduction to the principles, effects, and usage tips of Terastalization to help you take full advantage of this powerful mechanic.

What is Terastalization?

Terastalization is a phenomenon that occurs in the Paldea region, where Pokemon can crystallize and change their type. Each Pokemon has a unique Tera Type that can be activated during battle to change its type and enhance moves of that type.

How to Activate Terastalization

  • Each Pokemon can activate Terastalization once per battle
  • Terastalization lasts for 3 turns after activation
  • The Tera Type can be different from the Pokemon's original type
  • Moves of the Tera Type receive a 50% power boost

Terastalization Strategies

Offensive Terastalization

Use Terastalization to change your Pokemon's type to one that is super effective against the opponent, and use moves of that type to deal maximum damage.

Defensive Terastalization

Use Terastalization to change your Pokemon's type to one that resists the opponent's moves, reducing the damage received.

Coverage Terastalization

Use Terastalization to cover your Pokemon's weaknesses, changing to a type that resists moves that would normally deal heavy damage.

Advanced Tips

  • Learn each Pokemon's Tera Type to plan strategies in advance
  • Combine Terastalization with Mega Evolution for devastating power
  • Activate Terastalization at the right time to maximize its effect
  • Consider type matchups before activating Terastalization

Terastalization is a powerful mechanic that can change the course of any battle. Master Terastalization and become a battle master!

Multiplayer Terastal Battle Guide

March 1, 2026

Multiplayer Terastal battles are an innovative gameplay feature in PocketChampion that requires coordination and strategy between players. This guide introduces the rules, tips, and best team compositions for multiplayer Terastal battles to help you win in team battles.

Multiplayer Terastal Battle Rules

  • Each team consists of 4 players
  • Each player controls 2 Pokemon
  • Battles take place on a 4v4 field
  • Each Pokemon can activate Terastalization once per battle
  • The team that eliminates all opponent Pokemon wins

Team Roles

Tank

Tanks are responsible for absorbing damage and protecting teammates. They should have high defense and endurance, as well as moves that can protect or heal teammates.

Attacker

Attackers are responsible for dealing damage to enemies. They should have high attack power and speed, as well as moves that can deal massive damage.

Support

Support is responsible for helping teammates. They should have moves that can heal, protect, or enhance teammates.

Controller

Controllers are responsible for controlling the battlefield. They should have moves that can weaken, paralyze, or confuse enemies.

Team Strategies

Type Synergy

Ensure your team has good type diversity to cover each Pokemon's weaknesses and have advantages against various types of enemies.

Terastalization Coordination

Coordinate with your teammates to activate Terastalization at the right time, creating devastating combos that can quickly eliminate enemies.

Communication

Communication is key in multiplayer battles. Make sure to communicate with your teammates to coordinate attacks, defense, and strategies.

Advanced Tips

  • Learn the advantages and disadvantages of each type to plan strategies in advance
  • Use Terastalization to cover your team's weaknesses
  • Combine Terastalization with Mega Evolution for devastating power
  • Adjust your strategy based on the enemy team composition

Multiplayer Terastal battles are an exciting experience that requires teamwork and strategy. Gather your team and master multiplayer Terastal battles!

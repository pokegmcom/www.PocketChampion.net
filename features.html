Game Features

Explore PocketChampion's unique gameplay and exciting content

1-9 Generations 1000+ Pokemon Collection

PocketChampion includes all Pokemon from generations 1-9, over 1000 in total! You can choose any starter from any generation as your initial partner and start your adventure journey. Whether it's the classic Charmander, Bulbasaur, Squirtle, or the latest Sprigatito, Fuecoco, Quaxly, they can all be found in the game.

  • Choose from 1-9 generation starters
  • Collect over 1000 Pokemon
  • Complete evolution chains and form changes

Terastalization + Mega Evolution

PocketChampion innovatively combines the Terastalization and Mega Evolution systems, making battle strategies richer and more diverse. You can choose the appropriate evolution method based on the battle situation and create your own unique battle style.

  • Terastalization system, change Pokemon attributes
  • Mega Evolution, enhance Pokemon abilities
  • Strategic matching, create unique battle style

3D HD/Pixelated Free Switching

PocketChampion supports free switching between two visual styles: 3D HD graphics and pixelated style, allowing you to freely choose between modern visual effects and nostalgic classic style. At the same time, the battle perspective can also be freely adjusted, bringing you a brand new gaming experience.

  • 3D HD graphics, exquisite and detailed
  • Pixelated style, nostalgic classic
  • Free battle perspective adjustment

Two-player/Multiplayer Terastal Battles

PocketChampion offers various battle modes, including traditional two-player battles and innovative multiplayer Terastal battles. You can team up with friends, challenge powerful opponents, and experience the fun of teamwork.

  • Traditional two-player battles
  • Multiplayer Terastal battles
  • Team cooperation, strategic coordination
